# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Lumiverse is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community. We
expect contributors, maintainers, and community members to collaborate in good
faith and treat one another with professionalism and care.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and open to good-faith feedback.
- Focus discussion on ideas, code, and project outcomes—not personal attacks.
- Respect differing experiences, identities, backgrounds, and viewpoints.
- Protect private, sensitive, and security-related information.
- Help keep community spaces safe and productive.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or threats.
- Insults, slurs, sexualized language, or unwanted personal attention.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or project work.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Abusive, deceptive, or unsafe use of project spaces or systems.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct concerns privately to **[conduct contact or email]**.
Reports will be handled promptly, discreetly, and as fairly as possible.
Retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report is not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including a warning, removal of
content, temporary restrictions, or removal from project spaces, depending on
the severity and context of the conduct.
