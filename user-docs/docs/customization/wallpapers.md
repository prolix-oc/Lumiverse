# Wallpapers

Set background images or videos behind your chat to enhance the atmosphere.

---

## Setting a Wallpaper

### Global Wallpaper

A wallpaper that appears in all chats:

1. Open the **Wallpaper Panel**
2. Click **Upload** under the global section
3. Select an image or video file
4. The wallpaper appears behind all your chats

### Per-Chat Wallpaper

A wallpaper specific to one conversation:

1. Open the **Wallpaper Panel** while in a chat
2. Click **Upload** under the per-chat section
3. The wallpaper only appears in this specific chat

Per-chat wallpapers override the global wallpaper.

---

## Supported Formats

| Type | Formats | Size Limit |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Images** | PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF | Standard upload limits |
| **Videos** | MP4, WebM | 100 MB |

Videos loop automatically and play silently in the background.

---

## Removing a Wallpaper

Click **Remove** in the Wallpaper Panel to clear the current wallpaper (global or per-chat).

---

## Combining with Image Generation

If you have image generation enabled, AI-generated scene images can serve as dynamic wallpapers that update as the story progresses. The **background opacity** setting in the Image Generation panel controls how prominently the generated image appears.

---

## Tips

!!! tip "Use subtle images"
    Dark, low-contrast images work best as wallpapers — they set the mood without making text hard to read. The chat overlay provides some contrast, but busy images can still be distracting.

!!! tip "Match the theme"
    Pair your wallpaper with a matching theme. A cozy firelit image pairs well with warm accent colors; a starfield pairs with cool blues.
